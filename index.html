About Us

Established in 2016 in mainland China, Möge Tee has expanded globally,
with a significant presence in North America, Europe, Australia, and Southeast
Asia. Operating as Möge Tee ME&AFRICA Franchise, we've become the Middle
East and Africa general agent since 2020, solidifying our roots in the Middle
East market. Our mission is to introduce the Asian bubble tea trend to a wider
audience, transcending cultural boundaries and reshaping perceptions of this
traditionally enjoyed beverage. Complementing our "New-Style Tea" are delightful
Asian desserts like Japanese pancakes and cream mochi, adding a sweet twist to
our diverse menu. With a commitment to quality, all our products are crafted
fresh daily, featuring handcrafted loose leaf tea, fresh fruits, and dairy.

Möge Tee aims to make its products accessible to diverse communities, striving to
cater to all demographics with a range of high-quality, made-to-order items that
showcase visible quality and delectable freshness.
